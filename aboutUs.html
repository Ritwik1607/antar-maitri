About Us

Our Story

Driven by a shared passion for mental well-being,Antar Maitri was born to create a space where inspirational videos and songs converge, fostering a community that prioritizes mental health through mindful content.

Our Mission

At Antar Maitri, our mission is to curate and share a diverse collection of videos and songs that uplift, inspire, and contribute to the collective journey of fostering positive mental health and well-being
